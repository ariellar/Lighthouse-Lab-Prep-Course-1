Cranberry Bread and Butter Pudding with Grand Marnier Sauce

directions

For the Pudding

  1. Combine the milk, sugar, whites and yolks then mix and strain the mixture.
  2. Cut the crusts of about 12 pieces of bread and then cut into half to create triangles. Set these aside.
  3. Use the rest of the bread and cut into cubes, and make a layer of bread in the dish.
  4. Pour some of your liquid mixture on top and toss in some cranberries.
  5. Arrange the triangle slices of bread to your liking as the top layer of your pudding.
  6. Brush on some butter and sprinkle a few more cranberries and chocolate on top

For the Sauce

  1. Combine the cream, sugar and vanilla bean into a heavy bottomed pot and scald.
  2. Turn the burner to Low and pour 1/4 of the hot liquid into the yolks, whisk until combined.
  3. Quickly pour the egg mixture back into the hot cream remembering to continuously mix (tempering).
  4. Continue to cook this over Low heat stirring constantly until the mixture coats the back of the spoon.
  5. Immediately take the sauce from the burner, strain and add Grand Marnier to taste.

ingredients

For the Pudding

  • 4 Cups Homo Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 8 Eggs
  • 20 Slices of White Bread
  • 1 Cup Dried Cranberries
  • chocolate chips to taste
  • 1/4 Cup Melted Butter
  • 1 Large casserole dish 14 inches in diameter

For the Sauce

  • 1 Cup 36% Cream
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 Vanilla Bean
  • 3 Egg Yolks
  • Grand Marnier to Taste

Serve warm in a sauce boat. It is to be spooned over the warm pudding
